# Smoke — Premium Quit-Smoking App

A modern, premium consumer health app that helps people quit smoking. Aesthetic reference: Apple Fitness, Headspace, Whoop, Fitbit — clean, data-rich, calming yet motivating.

## Mood
- Deep near-black background (#0A0A1A) with subtle violet ambient depth.
- Premium dark glass surfaces with very soft borders and gentle shadows.
- Vibrant but tasteful accents: violet #8B5CF6 (primary), cyan #06B6D4 (secondary), emerald #10B981 (success/health), amber #F59E0B (warning).
- Generous whitespace, strong typographic hierarchy, large bold numbers.

## Components
- Rounded cards (radius 20-28) with 1px hairline borders and soft elevation.
- Big circular progress rings and arcs for the main day-counter.
- 2x2 stat tiles with a small line icon, a large 800-weight number, and a quiet label.
- Health-recovery vertical timeline with connectors and achieved/locked states.
- Crisp line icons (NOT emoji) everywhere for UI chrome.
- Bottom tab bar with a central circular floating action button.
- Subtle gradient hero card for the main metric.

## Don'ts
- No emoji as UI icons. No clutter. No flat lifeless gray boxes. No childish look.
