Privacy Policy

At Smoke, we value your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Data We Collect

Required Data

  • Account information: Email address, password (hashed)
  • Profile information: Username (optional)
  • Journey data: Quit date, daily cigarette count, pack price

Optional Data

  • Craving records: Date, intensity, trigger (only if you enter it)
  • Community posts: Anonymous or named sharing (your choice)
  • Daily check-in: Mood and energy level
  • Breathing exercises: Completed exercise duration

Automatically Collected Data

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage duration (anonymous)
  • Error reports (no personal data)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To track your quit-smoking journey and provide personal statistics
  • To verify and secure your account
  • To send milestone and badge notifications
  • To improve the app using anonymous, aggregated data
  • To provide a personalized in-app experience

3. Data Sharing

  • Your data is never sold or shared with any advertising networks.
  • Your data is not shared with third parties except as required by law.
  • The app's technical infrastructure runs on Supabase (Singapore).

4. Data Security

  • All data is transmitted with SSL/TLS encryption
  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt and never stored in plain text
  • The database is protected with Row Level Security (RLS); each user can only access their own data
  • API keys are stored on-device using Secure Store

5. Children's Privacy

Smoke is designed for individuals aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

6. Deleting Your Account

You can permanently delete all your data via Profile > Delete Account in the app. You may also contact us at the email below to request deletion within 30 days.
